Our Mission

First-Generation Investment Group (FGIG) is a student-led organization at the University of Michigan dedicated to empowering first-generation and socioeconomically underrepresented students to break into elite finance and consulting careers.

We believe that drive, grit, and potential—not pedigree—define success.

What We Offer

  • Technical training in finance and consulting fundamentals
  • Mentorship from industry professionals and Ross upperclassmen
  • A close-knit community of like-minded peers

Why We Exist

FGIG exists to level the playing field, redefine who gets access, and prove that talent can come from anywhere.
